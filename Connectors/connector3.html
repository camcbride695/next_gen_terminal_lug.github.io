Connector Option 2

The busbar style connector can be used for grounding or energized purposes. In order to be used for grounding purposes it must be mounted to the aircraft at the desired ground location. The busbar design shape and number of wire connections can be modified. It can have as few as two or as many as safely can be connected. The benefit with these is the ability to ground multiple things with one attachment to the plane. These would still require a tool (screwdriver or allen wrench) and torque specification check
