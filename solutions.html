Possible Solutions

Wago Connection

The Wago style design stems from personal experience using the “Wago” brand connectors in residential and commercial electrical systems. The Wago connector is essentially a lever operated device that terminates wires together. These connectors come in several different sizes, ranging from 2 wires to upwards of 10 wires. This connector will essentially remove several processes currently required and reduce the tools necessary for making connections.

Ball Dent

By utilizing a crimp connection with a ball shaped end, they can be placed in an array, similar to their existing plugs for other connections, or grounded individually by a clothespin like mechanism. To lock the clothespin like mechanism, a locking bar can be equipped and attached to the mounting posts.

Double Lug

This connection would be the same as the current connection, but it would allow for more connections to be grounded at one point, thus saving time.

Bus Bar

Similar to what is found in breaker panels in residential applications, a bus bar is a row of holes that bare wire is inserted into and clamped perpendicularly by a screw. Crimp sleeves may be required as not to damage the small grounding wires.
