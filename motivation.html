Motivation Schedule Research About Us

Motivation

Goal

Currently, Gulfstream has a functional way to ground the power systems of the aircraft. The goal of our research is to pave the way for a new procedure for grounding the power systems to be made. The criteria for ideas must try to reduce cost, decrease installation time, and remain ethical in nature.

Cost

Currently, the total cost per aircraft at eight minutes per part installation is approximately $30,719.04. New possible parts must try to reduce this cost, reducing the cost per part would be one way to reduce the total cost.

Time

Another way to reduce overall cost per aircraft would be to reduce the installation time per part. Currently, installation is at about eight minutes a part. Gulfstream has expressed insterest in making the new connections tooless installs to help with installation times.

Ethics

While it is important to Gulfstream and our group to make a new connection that is cheap and a quick install, we must not forget to remember ethical engineering. Currently each terminal lug is torqued and inspected with two people to ensure proper connections. Our new idea could be tooless, even if it is not torqued, it needs to be just as secure to ensure customer safety.
